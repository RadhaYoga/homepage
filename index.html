Innovative Techniques and Offerings

Making every session joyful and welcoming

Creative Program Designs

Radha designs classes around fun themes like "Yoga with Props," "Facial Yoga," and "Aqua Yoga". She also integrates flute music and mantras to set the tone and pace of sessions.

Personalized & Group Routines

She provides personalized attention in both group and private sessions. She tailors modifications to accommodate different skill levels and physical conditions.

Gamification of Fitness

Radha incorporates fun challenges, like holding a plank or mastering a specific pose, to motivate her students. Her goal is to keep the sessions engaging and fun.
